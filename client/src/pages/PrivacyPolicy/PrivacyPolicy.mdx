## Muninnfy Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is effective from 05/04/2026.

Muninnfy (“we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

By using Muninnfy, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy and our <Link className='link' to={'/terms-of-service'}>Terms of Service</Link>.

### 1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve Muninnfy.

#### Information you provide:

- Account information (such as username, email address)
- Content you create (such as wishlists, comments, and other submissions)
- Communications you send to us (such as support emails)

#### Automatically collected information:

- Device and browser information
- IP address and general location data
- Usage data (such as interactions with features, pages visited, and session activity)

#### Cookies and similar technologies:

We use cookies and similar technologies for authentication, security, and improving the user experience.
For more details, see our <Link className='link' to={'/cookie-policy'}>Cookie Policy</Link>.

### 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide, operate, and maintain Muninnfy
- Authenticate users and secure accounts
- Personalize your experience
- Improve features, performance, and usability
- Communicate with you (including support and important updates)
- Analyze usage trends and develop insights

We may also use data in aggregated or anonymized form for analytics, research, and service improvement.

### 3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share information in the following cases:

- Service providers: With trusted third-party providers who help operate Muninnfy (e.g., hosting, analytics), under appropriate safeguards
- Legal requirements: If required to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes
- Protection of rights: To protect the rights, safety, and integrity of Muninnfy, our users, or others
- Aggregated or anonymized Data: We may share data that does not identify you personally

If we ever plan to share personally identifiable information in new ways, we will notify you in advance.

### 4. User Content and Privacy Controls

- You control the visibility of your wishlists (private, public, or followers-only)
- Content you choose to make public may be visible to others
- Even when content is deleted, some data may remain in backups or logs for operational or legal reasons

### 5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to:

- Provide the service
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

When you delete your account, your data will be removed or anonymized, except where retention is required for legitimate purposes.

### 6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

### 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Object to or restrict certain processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at: support@muninnfy.com.

### 8. Children's Privacy

Muninnfy is not intended for children under the age of 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

If we become aware that such data has been collected, we will take steps to delete it.
