### 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website or use an app. They help websites recognize your device and store information about your preferences or activity.

### 2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

- Enhance user experience

### 3. Types of Cookies We Use

These cookies are necessary for Muninnfy to function properly. They enable core features such as authentication, security, and session management.

#### Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how users interact with Muninnfy, such as which features are used most often. This information is typically aggregated and does not directly identify you.

These cookies allow us to remember your preferences and improve your experience.
